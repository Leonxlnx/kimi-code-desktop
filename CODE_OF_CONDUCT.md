# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors and maintainers must make participation respectful, constructive, and free from harassment.

Examples of expected behavior include:

- Giving specific and useful technical feedback
- Respecting different backgrounds, experience levels, and viewpoints
- Accepting correction and focusing on the project rather than the person
- Protecting private account, security, and workspace information

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, personal attacks, discriminatory language, deliberate disclosure of private information, or sustained disruption of project work.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this standard. Temporary or permanent participation restrictions may be applied when necessary.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through the contact options on the [Leonxlnx GitHub profile](https://github.com/Leonxlnx). Do not include credentials, private source files, or unrelated personal information.

Maintainers will review reports in confidence, avoid unnecessary disclosure, and respond in proportion to the behavior and its impact.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository spaces and in public spaces when someone is representing the project.
